## LEADERS (anchor, enriched)
- **Mullá Ḥusayn-i-Bushrú'í** (Bábu'l-Báb) · Bábí · hoisted the **Black Standard**, led **202 disciples** to Quddús in the Jazíriy-i-Khaḍrá; clashed at Bárfurúsh (Sa‘ídu'l-‘Ulamá's incitement); **killed at dawn 9 Rabí‘u'l-Avval 1265**, shot by ‘Abbás-Qulí Khán-i-Láríjání in a night sortie.
- **Quddús** (Muḥammad-‘Alíy-i-Bárfurúshí) · Bábí · 18th Letter; **sole command after Mullá Ḥusayn's death**; martyred + dismembered in the Bárfurúsh square (Sa‘ídu'l-‘Ulamá); dying prayer of forgiveness (DB 21308).
