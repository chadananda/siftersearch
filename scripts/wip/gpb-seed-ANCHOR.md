## ⚠ CONSOLIDATED NAMESAKE FIREWALLS (apply to EVERY Dawn-Breakers name)
- **Bábu'l-Báb** = Mullá Ḥusayn-i-Bushrú'í, NOT the Báb.
- **Yaḥyá:** Vaḥíd (Siyyid Yaḥyáy-i-Dárábí, Bábí hero, Nayríz) ≠ Mírzá Yaḥyá (Ṣubḥ-i-Azal, opponent) ≠ John the Baptist.
- **Muḥammad-‘Alí cluster:** Quddús (Bárfurúshí, Letter) · Ḥujjat (Zanjání, hero) · Mírzá Muḥammad-‘Alí (the breaker) · the Báb's given name ‘Alí-Muḥammad — all distinct.
- **The Báqirs:** Mullá Báqir-i-Tabrízí (Letter, relic-bearer → Bahá'í) ≠ Muḥammad-Báqir-i-Bushrú'í (Letter, Mullá Ḥusayn's nephew → Ṭabarsí martyr) ≠ Qumshih convert ≠ Ustád Báqir of Káshán ≠ Áqá Muḥammad-Báqir-i-Qahvih-chí ≠ Ḥájí Siyyid Muḥammad-Báqir-i-Rashtí (Iṣfahán mujtahid).
- **The two Mullá ‘Alís:** Basṭámí (martyr, ‘Iráq) ≠ Qúchání/Razí (natural death).
- **Siyyid Káẓim:** Rashtí (Shaykhí head) ≠ Zanjání (Báb's companion).
- **3 Ḥusayn Kháns:** Niẓámu'd-Dawlih/Ájúdán-Báshí (Shíráz governor, Báb-era) ≠ Mírzá Ḥusayn Khán Mushíru'd-Dawlih (Constantinople ambassador, Bahá'u'lláh-era) ≠ Bábí martyrs named Ḥusayn.
- **2 Tabríz Shaykhu'l-Isláms:** Mámáqání (warrant) ≠ Mírzá ‘Alí Aṣghar (bastinado).
- **3 Branches:** Ghusn-i-A‘ẓam (Most Great = 'Abdu'l-Bahá) ≠ Ghusn-i-Akbar (Greater = Muḥammad-‘Alí breaker) ≠ Ghusn-i-Athar (Purest = Mírzá Mihdí).
- **2 Leaves:** Most Exalted Leaf (Navváb) ≠ Greatest Holy Leaf (Bahíyyih).
- **2 Antichrists:** Bábí Rev = Ḥájí Mírzá Áqásí (Balyuzi's term) ≠ Bahá'í Rev = Siyyid Muḥammad-i-Iṣfahání (GPB).
- **2 Nabíls:** A‘ẓam (Zarandí, Dawn-Breakers author) ≠ Akbar (Qá'iní, Hand).
- **2 Badí‘s:** Mírzá Badí‘u'lláh (breaker) ≠ Badí‘ (Áqá Buzurg, martyr, Tablet-bearer "Pride of Martyrs").
- **Fáṭimih:** shared name — Ṭáhirih (the Letter) by context; never drop.
- **‘Abbás:** ‘Abbás Effendi (='Abdu'l-Bahá) ≠ ‘Abbás Ḥilmí Páshá II (Khedive) ≠ ‘Abbás-Qulí Khán-i-Láríjání (killed Mullá Ḥusayn).
- **Olga ≠ Mignon:** Princess Olga (of Greece, regent Paul's wife) ≠ Marie's daughter Mignon (Queen of Yugoslavia).
