- d. 29 Apr 1912. Successor named: Mírzá Hádíy-i-Dawlat-Ábádí (apostate).
- aliases: Ṣubḥ-i-Azal ("Morning of Eternity"), Ḥaḍrat-i-Azal · Mírzá Yaḥyá Núrí · Azal/Azalí (partial).
- MERGE fragment IDs: 614811, 614812, 625411(Azal), 623254, 614818, 624044, 638251, 633578, 658135, 642025, 649214 + many. ⚠ "Azalí/Azalis" (615160,614781,632777,646867,…) = the FOLLOWERS → keep SEPARATE group entity.
- traps: NOT Vaḥíd (Siyyid Yaḥyá-i-Dárábí, loyal Nayríz hero); NOT John the Baptist; NOT Yaḥyá Dawlatábádí.
