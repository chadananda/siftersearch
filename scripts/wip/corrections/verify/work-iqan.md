## DESCRIBE
The Kitáb-i-Íqán ("Book of Certitude"), a major doctrinal work of Bahá'u'lláh revealed in Baghdád in 1861 in response to questions posed by the Báb's maternal uncle. It expounds progressive revelation, the station of the Prophets, and the symbolic interpretation of scripture, and is regarded as the foremost doctrinal/theological work of the Bahá'í Faith after the Kitáb-i-Aqdas.
