## ⚠ FIREWALLS CARRIED FROM THE DEEP PASS (do not re-merge)

1. **"Bayán" five-way polysemy** — Persian Bayán [work, BB00001] · Arabic Bayán [work, BB00020] · the Revelation of the Báb [concept] · the people/community of the Bayán = the Bábís [concept] · generic "utterance/exposition" [not an entity]. See DEEP §"BAYÁN FIVE-WAY".
2. **Dalá'il-i-Sab‘ih (Seven PROOFS, apologetic, Stage 3, BB00015) ≠ Khaṣá'il-i-Sab‘ih (Seven QUALIFICATIONS/Directives, ordinances, Stage 1, OL 224).** The single most likely merge-error.
3. **Ṣaḥífih-i-‘Adlíyyih ≡ Risáliy-i-‘Adlíyyih** (one work, root principles, BB00017) — but **≠ Risáliy-i-Furú‘-i-‘Adlíyyih** (branches, BB00039).
4. **Misattributions stripped:** Kitáb-i-Aqdas is Bahá'u'lláh's (NOT the Báb); "Commentary on the Ṣád of Ṣamad" is Quddús's (≠ the Báb's own Súriy-i-Tawḥíd, corpus 20363 / OL 026).
5. **Persian↔Arabic genitive equivalence:** Tafsír-i-Súriy-i-Kawthar = Tafsir-i-Suratu'l-Kawthar; Tafsír-i-Súriy-i-‘Aṣr = Tafsír-i-Súriy-i-Va'l-‘Aṣr; "Kitáb bayn al-Ḥaramayn" = Ṣaḥífiy-i-Bayni'l-Ḥaramayn.
