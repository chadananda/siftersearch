Tried/excommunicated at Najaf/Karbilá; Balyuzi 466 id 6715807: "the first martyr… put to death somewhere in ‘Iráq." merge: 614852,641741,641698,628156. FULL.

DB: the Báb urged Mírzá Yaḥyá to learn the Writings from him. Fellow-prisoner of Bahá'u'lláh in the Síyáh-Chál.
